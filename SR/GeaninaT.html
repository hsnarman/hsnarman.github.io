The Project - Automated IP Reputation Analyzer System

Over the summer of 2018 I have developed a cross-checking system which automatically analyzes a number of reliable blocklist databases and assign a weighted security degree of domains and IP addresses to inform users and applications about possibilities of malicious activities. My mentor: Dr. Husnu Narman. Now, during the fall semester of 2018, I am working with my mentor and fellow group member Jared Lewis on extending this project by adding the machine learning tool. The demand for this is high, as its impossible for blacklists to locate every single malicious domain. On this web page, I will be recording a weekly blog of my research as well as posting the resources I have been using to develop this project.
